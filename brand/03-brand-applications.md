# Grupo Aia — Brand Applications

---

## 1. Business Cards

### Front
- **Layout:** Minimal, left-aligned
- **Background:** Cream (#F5F0E8) card stock, uncoated matte finish
- **Top-left:** Logo (script "Aia" + pillar words) in Rich Black
- **Bottom-left block:**
  - Name in Cormorant Garamond Medium, 10pt, Rich Black
  - Title in Inter Regular, 8pt, Graphite
  - Thin 0.5pt Signature Gold horizontal rule (20mm wide)
  - Phone in Inter Regular, 8pt, Charcoal
  - Email in Inter Regular, 8pt, Charcoal
  - Website in Inter Regular, 8pt, Charcoal

### Back
- **Background:** Rich Black (#1A1A1A) — full bleed
- **Center:** Script "Aia" in Signature Gold (#B8964B), at 25mm width
- **Bottom-center:** Tagline "Guardiães da Alma" in Cormorant Garamond Light Italic, 8pt, white, tracked +0.1em

### Specifications
- **Size:** 90mm x 55mm (European standard)
- **Paper:** 400gsm cotton card stock, soft-touch matte lamination on front, uncoated back
- **Print:** Offset, 4/4 (full color both sides)
- **Special finish:** Signature Gold script on back should be matte gold foil stamped for premium runs — this replicates the textured gold quality of the digital logo in physical form

**Strategic rationale:** The card is itself an act of Acolhimento. The weight of the stock, the soft-touch finish, the unhurried layout — it communicates care before a word is read. The dual-sided color treatment (light/dark) represents the brand's integration of openness and depth.

## 2. Letterhead & Stationery System

### Letterhead (A4)

- **Paper:** 120gsm cream uncoated stock
- **Top-left (25mm from edges):** Full logo in Rich Black, 40mm wide
- **Top-right (25mm from right edge):** Single decorative signature sweep in Signature Gold, 5% opacity, anchored to top-right corner — spans 60mm
- **Body text area:** Starts 55mm from top, 25mm margins left/right
- **Footer (15mm from bottom):**
  - Thin 0.5pt Signature Gold rule spanning full text width
  - Below rule: Address, phone, email, website in Inter Regular, 7.5pt, Graphite, separated by em dashes
  - Registration/legal information in Inter Regular, 6.5pt, Stone

### Continuation Sheet
- Same paper stock and margins
- No logo — only the subtle signature sweep watermark in top-right at 3% opacity
- Footer identical to page one

### Envelope (DL — 220mm x 110mm)
- **Paper:** 120gsm cream uncoated, matching letterhead
- **Front:** Full logo, top-left corner, 30mm wide, Rich Black
- **Back flap:** Sweep icon centered, Signature Gold, 8mm wide
- **Return address:** Inter Regular, 7pt, Graphite, bottom-left of back

### Notecard (A6 — 148mm x 105mm)
- **Front:** Full logo centered, 25mm wide, Rich Black
- **Inside:** Blank cream stock for handwritten notes
- **Back:** Sweep icon centered, Signature Gold, 6mm wide
- **Envelope:** Matching cream, unlined

**Strategic rationale:** Physical correspondence carries disproportionate weight for the 40–90 demographic. This audience values tangible communication. The stationery system is designed to feel personal and unhurried — a letter from Aia should feel like receiving a letter from someone who cares, not a form from an institution.

## 3. Email Signature

```
—

Dr. [Name]
[Title] | Grupo Aia

T: +55 (XX) XXXX-XXXX
E: name@grupoaia.com.br
W: grupoaia.com.br

Alma · Integração · Acolhimento
```

### Design Specifications
- **Separator:** Em dash on its own line, Charcoal
- **Name:** Inter SemiBold, Rich Black (#1A1A1A)
- **Title & Company:** Inter Regular, Graphite
- **Contact details:** Inter Regular, Charcoal, each on its own line
- **Tagline:** Cormorant Garamond Italic, Signature Gold (#B8964B), interpuncts between words
- **Logo:** NOT included in email signature (degrades in rendering; the text tagline carries the brand)
- **Links:** Rich Black, no underline until hover

**Strategic rationale:** Clean, readable, loads instantly in all email clients. The pillar words in Cormorant Garamond Italic add brand character without an image dependency. No social media icons — the brand doesn't chase attention.

## 4. Social Media Profile Templates

### Instagram

**Avatar:**
- Script "Aia" on Rich Black circle background
- Script in Signature Gold, centered
- Minimum 320x320px

**Story highlight covers:**
- Rich Black background
- Single thin-line icon per cover in Signature Gold
- Categories: Alma (flame icon), Integração (woven lines icon), Acolhimento (open hands icon), Equipe (people icon), Espaço (arch icon)

**Post templates (3 core types):**
- **Quote card:** Cream background, Cormorant Garamond Light text in Rich Black, thin Signature Gold sweep as decorative element, source attribution in Inter
- **Photo with text overlay:** Photography with 70% opacity Rich Black strip at bottom, Inter Medium white text
- **Educational carousel:** Cream background, Rich Black text, Antique Bronze accent elements, page numbers in Signature Gold

### Facebook

**Avatar:** Same as Instagram
**Cover image (820x312px):**
- Full-width lifestyle photograph (warm, contemplative mood)
- Semi-transparent Cream overlay on right third
- Full logo in Rich Black on overlay area, right-aligned
- Tagline in Cormorant Garamond Italic below logo

### LinkedIn

**Avatar:** Same as Instagram
**Cover image (1128x191px):**
- Split design: left 60% photography (hands, garden, reading), right 40% Rich Black solid
- Full logo in white on Rich Black area
- Three pillar words stacked in Cormorant Garamond Light Italic, Signature Gold, below logo

### YouTube

**Avatar:** Same as Instagram
**Channel banner (2560x1440px):**
- Full-width photography (warm, contemplative)
- Bottom gradient from transparent to Rich Black
- Full logo in white, bottom-center
- Tagline in Cormorant Garamond Light, Cream, below logo

### WhatsApp Business

**Avatar:** Script "Aia" on Rich Black (matches all platforms)
**Status banner:** Tagline "Guardiães da Alma" in Cormorant Garamond on Cream background

**Strategic rationale for social media:** The avatar uses the script "Aia" — its flowing calligraphic form is distinctive even at small sizes. Rich Black backgrounds with gold script create instant recognition in feeds. Content templates prioritize readability and warmth over trend-chasing design. The system is designed for the platforms the 40–90 audience actually uses (WhatsApp and Facebook over TikTok and X).

## 5. Presentation Template

### Software: Google Slides / PowerPoint / Keynote (provide all three)
### Aspect ratio: 16:9
### Base colors: Cream background, Rich Black text, Signature Gold accents

---

### Slide 1: Title Slide

- **Background:** Cream (#F5F0E8)
- **Left half:** Presentation title in Cormorant Garamond Regular, 44pt, Rich Black. Subtitle in Inter Regular, 18pt, Graphite. Date and presenter name in Inter Regular, 14pt, Stone.
- **Right half:** Signature sweep decorative element, Signature Gold, 3% opacity, anchored to top-right corner
- **Bottom-left:** Full logo, 30mm, Rich Black
- **Bottom-right:** Confidentiality notice if needed, Inter Regular, 10pt, Stone

### Slide 2: Section Divider

- **Background:** Rich Black (#1A1A1A) — full bleed
- **Center:** Section title in Cormorant Garamond Light, 48pt, white
- **Subtitle:** Inter Regular, 18pt, Champagne
- **Decorative:** Three thin arcs radiating from bottom-left corner, Signature Gold, 10% opacity

### Slide 3: Content Slide

- **Background:** Cream
- **Top-left:** Section title in Cormorant Garamond Medium, 28pt, Rich Black
- **Below title:** 0.5pt Signature Gold rule, width of title text
- **Body area:** Two-column or single-column layout. Inter Regular, 16pt, Charcoal. 1.5 line spacing.
- **Bullet style:** Signature Gold filled circles, 6pt, aligned to Inter baseline
- **Bottom-right:** Page number in Inter Regular, 10pt, Stone. Sweep icon, 6mm, Stone, 20% opacity, next to page number.

### Slide 4: Data / Chart Slide

- **Background:** Cream
- **Chart area:** Centered, 70% of slide width
- **Chart colors:** Rich Black (primary data), Signature Gold (highlight/comparison), Antique Bronze (tertiary), Blush Clay (quaternary)
- **Chart style:** Clean, minimal. No gridlines. Subtle axis lines in Mist. Data labels in Inter Medium, 12pt.
- **Chart title:** Cormorant Garamond Medium, 24pt, Rich Black, left-aligned above chart
- **Source/footnote:** Inter Regular, 10pt, Stone, bottom-left

### Slide 5: Quote Slide

- **Background:** Cream
- **Large opening quotation mark:** Cormorant Garamond Light, 120pt, Signature Gold, 20% opacity
- **Quote text:** Cormorant Garamond Regular Italic, 28pt, Rich Black, centered, max 3 lines
- **Attribution:** Inter Medium, 14pt, Graphite, centered below quote with em dash prefix

### Slide 6: Closing Slide

- **Background:** Rich Black
- **Center:** Full logo in white, generous size (50% of slide width)
- **Below logo:** Tagline "Guardiães da Alma" in Cormorant Garamond Light Italic, 20pt, Signature Gold
- **Bottom-center:** Contact information in Inter Regular, 12pt, Champagne

**Strategic rationale:** Presentations are high-stakes touchpoints — investor meetings, referral partner pitches, conference talks. The template must project clinical credibility (clean data slides) AND emotional resonance (quote slides, warm colors). Cream replaces white for warmth on projectors. Large type sizes ensure readability in conference rooms. The system is restrained enough to let content lead.
